    Project Three

    Project Four

  • L13: Learning Portfolio Milestones 3

    • I used feedback from students on Kritik to improve my portfolio. My goal was to make it more organized and visually appealing.

    Reflection: In my final project, I discovered something useful. By breaking my portfolio into several pages, it became much easier for people to navigate compared to just having one long page. Also, sharing full links to my assignments, instead of just small images, made it simpler for everyone to access and understand them. Through this experience, I learned how crucial it is to keep information clear and easy to find.
