Assignment Four

  • L03: 3D Printing

I printed a Nittany Lion figure with 3DPrinterOS and had the option to either personalize it or choose another object. I picked up the final print from the Media Commons at Paterno.

Reflection: I found it fun getting a physical object from my classwork. Compared to my past experience with AutoCAD, 3DPrinterOS felt more user-friendly and accessible. Reflection: I was surprised by how complex such a small device could be. I learned about the inner components, such as the I/O devices and memory, and how they all work together. This made me curious about how technology products are designed.
